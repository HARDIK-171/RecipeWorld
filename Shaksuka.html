Dhokla Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp oil

For Tempering:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, yogurt, water, lemon juice, sugar, salt, and turmeric powder. Mix well to form a smooth batter.
  2. Set the batter aside for 10-15 minutes to allow it to ferment slightly.
  3. Prepare a steamer and grease a dish that fits inside it.
  4. Just before steaming, add baking soda to the batter and stir gently to mix.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased dish and steam for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let the dhokla cool slightly, then cut into squares.
  7. For tempering, heat oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  8. Add water and sugar, stir, and pour this mixture over the steamed dhokla.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves, and serve warm with chutney.

Note: You can also garnish with grated coconut for added flavor.
