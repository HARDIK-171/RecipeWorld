Paneer Tikka Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion (cut into cubes)
  • 1 large capsicum (cut into cubes)
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, cumin powder, chaat masala, lemon juice, and salt. Mix well to form a marinade.
  2. Add the paneer cubes, onion, and capsicum to the marinade. Gently mix to coat the paneer and vegetables evenly. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F) or heat a grill pan on medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated paneer, onion, and capsicum onto skewers alternately.
  5. Place the skewers in the oven or on the grill pan. Brush with oil and cook for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally until the paneer is golden and slightly charred.
  6. Remove from the heat and sprinkle some chaat masala on top for extra flavor.
  7. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges. Enjoy your Paneer Tikka!
