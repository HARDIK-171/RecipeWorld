Dosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately. Soak the rice and poha together and urad dal with fenugreek seeds in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Drain the water and grind the rice and poha to a smooth batter. Grind the urad dal and fenugreek seeds separately until fluffy.
  3. Mix both batters together, add salt, and let the batter ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours.
  4. Once fermented, stir the batter. If it's too thick, add a little water to get a pouring consistency.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladleful of batter. Spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  6. Drizzle a few drops of oil around the edges and cook until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar. Enjoy your dosa!
