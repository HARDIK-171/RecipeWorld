Misal Pav Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sprouted moong beans or matki (moth beans)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1 large tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp goda masala or garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sev (fried gram flour noodles)
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 lemon (cut into wedges)
  • 4 pav (soft bread rolls)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown. Then, add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  3. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and goda masala (or garam masala). Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add sprouted moong beans or matki. Stir well and add salt to taste.
  5. Pour in water, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the beans are cooked.
  6. Once cooked, garnish with sev, chopped coriander, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  7. Serve hot with pav bread on the side. Enjoy your Misal Pav!
