Raita Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth.
  2. Add the chopped cucumber, roasted cumin powder, and salt to the yogurt.
  3. Mix everything well until combined.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  5. Serve chilled as a side dish with spicy meals or enjoy as a refreshing dip.
