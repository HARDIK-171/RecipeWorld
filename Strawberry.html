Strawberry custard is a simple and delicious dessert made by combining fresh strawberries with a smooth, creamy custard base. Hereâ€™s a quick overview:

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries: Fresh strawberries are ideal for the best flavor. They add natural sweetness and a slight tang.
  • Custard Powder (optional): Vanilla custard powder is often used to thicken and flavor the custard, though some recipes may use eggs and vanilla extract instead.
  • Milk: The base of the custard; whole milk or cream gives the best creamy texture.
  • Sugar: Adds sweetness, adjustable based on your preference.

Preparation:

  1. Prepare the Custard: In a pot, warm the milk and gradually add the custard powder mixed with a bit of cold milk to avoid lumps. Stir continuously until it thickens.
  2. Sweeten the Custard: Add sugar and continue to stir until it dissolves. Cook for a few more minutes until the custard reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Combine with Strawberries: Allow the custard to cool slightly, then fold in sliced strawberries. You can serve it warm or chilled.

Serving Suggestions:

  • Garnish with extra strawberries, whipped cream, or a sprig of mint.
  • Serve it in bowls or glasses for a nice presentation.
This dessert is refreshing, especially when chilled, and makes a lovely treat during warm months. It's perfect for strawberry lovers!
